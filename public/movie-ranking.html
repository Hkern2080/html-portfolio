Definitive Star Wars Trilogy Movie Ranking

Star Wars Movies Ranked from Best to Worst.

1. The Empire Strikes Back

The Best Star Wars of all time, there is no discussion or debate

2. A New Hope

The movie that started it all.

3. Revenge of the Sith

Anakin falls as do the Jedi, culminating in the epic battle between Master and Student.

4. Return of The Jedi

The Empire falls and Vader is redeemed, what's not to love.

5. Attack of the clones

Dooku puts an arrogant kid in his place and Yoda shows why he is the man.

6. The Force Awakens

Enjoyable movie but the Mary Sue plotline and lack of sensible character developement hurts what could have been a great film.

7. The Rise of Skywalker

Sorry Disney, Your colors are showing.

8. The Phantom Menace

We waited 16 years for this disappointment, well, at least Darth Maul was kinda cool.

9. The Last Jedi

Luke's character assassination hurt me in my soul. Snoke was a huge missed opportunity, Kylo shows himself to be much more like the bratty teen version of Anakin than the Fearsome Darth Vader he idolizes, and find a way to show Leia's power without turning her into Mary Poppins.
